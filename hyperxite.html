HyperXite EDS / Halbach

Active Maglev Experiment • Halbach Arrays • Mechanical Design

This project focused on the physical design and implementation of a passive electrodynamic suspension (EDS) system for magnetic levitation, utilizing Halbach arrays. I led the design and fabrication of the levitation test rig and magnetic pucks used to experimentally analyze lift, drag, and stability. All components were CAD-modeled in SolidWorks and manufactured via 3D printing and machining. The project was part of UCI’s Hyperloop team, HyperXite. Our subteam, Levitation R&D, won 1st place in Research Excellence at the 2025 Hyperloop Global Conference in Queen’s University, Canada.
